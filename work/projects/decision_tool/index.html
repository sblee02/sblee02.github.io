Decision making tool

Sep 2020 - Dec 2020 | Waterloo, Ontario CA

This team project was done during one of the management course. We had an opportunity to connect with management engineering alumni and build a desired tool for them. Our alumni was working at a finance and he requested for decision making tool which decides the best feature by comparing the cost, access, effectiveness. We applied management principles with Fishbone Diagram to foresee cause and effect of the project and developed the tool with Excel VBA to calculate the most efficient path from a given data table
