Internship @ Crowdworks Inc.

May 2021 - Dec 2021 | Seolleung, Seoul ROK

As my first internship ever, I had an opportunity to work an AI industry. Crowdworks Inc. was like a bridge between users and AI company(client). The company provided a platform to users and users would extra desired objects from sources. Users earn money by extracting number of objects from chosen project and the company sells the extracted objects to clients(mostly AI companies) who need test sets for machine learning purposes.

The role "CTO staff intern" may be a bit unfamiliar with many of you. Followed by the job description, the position was to assist Chief Technology Officer and it's department. I had 5 main tasks at Crowdworks Inc. during 8 months internship.

  1. Code Refactoring
  2. Data Analysis
  3. Network Manager
  4. QA
  5. Cooperative meeting organizer
